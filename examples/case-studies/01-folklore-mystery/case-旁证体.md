## What this case is

旁证体 is **the craft baseline of this lab** — a novel康康 hand-wrote from scratch (outline iteratively refined, prose hand-written). AI was minimally involved.

It is included here because the multi-agent workflow described in this lab (see `case-鲛人蛇吻.md`) was designed **to preserve and propagate** the craft choices visible in 旁证体. Without this baseline, the workflow has no target. With it, the workflow has something specific to be faithful to.

## Craft methodology (preview — full version forthcoming in `复盘-旁证体.md`)

The working set of craft methodologies surfaced from this baseline (in 执白--旁证体's own words, to be expanded in 复盘-旁证体.md):

1. **物件叙事链** — Objects carry load, not interiority. The same object recurs from Ch1 to Ch28, accumulating weight by repetition rather than commentary.
2. **双对照 (double-mirroring)** — Object cross-witness (互证屋 玻璃柜 vs. 家中 窗台) + CP 冰火 contrast + main-CP BE × side-CP HE foil.
3. **B 类记账** — A-line: visible plot beats. B-line: what's actually happening beneath the consciousness. B-line surfaces through objects, not exposition.
4. **工艺红线 (craft red lines)** — Restraint as gravity / no-explain past lives / HE-as-responsibility / etc. Hard constraints the author refuses to cross.
5. **"不是 X 是 X" 禁用** — The AI-default contrast-clause structure is rejected; direct action preferred.
6. **克制不写成 SOP** — Restraint surfaces through physical action (an extra ten seconds brushing teeth, a half-second pause on the top button), not through declarations like "trained to confirm in three seconds".
7. **章节物理钩子** — Same-object continuity from Ch24 end to Ch25 open ensures readers carry forward weight without summary.

These are the seven craft hooks the multi-agent workflow must preserve. They serve as the evaluation rubric for whether the workflow has "succeeded" on 鲛人蛇吻 v2.

## What this case is not

Not a story to be appreciated in isolation. The excerpt and methodology are **portfolio specimens** demonstrating that craft can be:

(a) **Hand-built** by an author with specific aesthetic commitments
(b) **Made explicit** as methodology, not left as tacit "good taste"
(c) **Used as a target** for AI-augmented workflows that must preserve it
