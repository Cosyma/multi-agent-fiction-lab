# Genre Bucket: psychological-character-study

> Status: **planned for v0.4** · placeholder directory.

This bucket will host case studies in the psychological character-study mode — fiction where the **central engine is internal-state development**, revealed through carefully paced disclosure of interiority rather than external plot mechanics.

Endorsed by both 执白 incarnations (2026-06-11) and ratified by author (2026-06-12) as the third genre bucket per [`DECISIONS.md`](../../../DECISIONS.md) D10.

## Why this bucket exists

Both 执白 incarnations independently noted that **旁证体 already demonstrates many of the craft patterns this bucket extends**: object-narrative chains as proxy for interiority, restraint as gravity, B-line accounting (what's actually happening beneath consciousness). The 03-psychological-character-study bucket takes these patterns and applies them to a domain where **the character's interior is the engine itself**, not a metaphor for an external event.

This is what makes this bucket different from 01-folklore-mystery (where the supernatural / mythic framework carries the engine) and 02-mystery (where the puzzle / social structure carries it). Here, **interiority is the load-bearing element**.

## Connection to 01-folklore-mystery (旁证体)

旁证体 from `01-folklore-mystery/case-旁证体.md` is the **craft baseline** for the entire lab. Its seven craft hooks — particularly **物件叙事链**, **B 类记账**, and **克制不写成 SOP** — overlap with what makes a psychological character study work as fiction.

The adaptation will involve more explicit `voice-check` tuning (distinguishing internal monologue vs external speech, distinguishing the character's self-narration vs the author's intrusion), and a tightening of `forbidden-check` against psychologized exposition (the trap of "the character did X because she had Y") — which most LLMs love and most good psychological fiction refuses.
